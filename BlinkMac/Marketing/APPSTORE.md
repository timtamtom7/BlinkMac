## Description (Short — 170 chars)
BlinkMac records one 30-second video every day, building a cinematic personal archive powered by AI.

## Description (Full)
Every day, BlinkMac captures one 30-second moment — automatically. No editing, no decisions, just your life in motion.

**How it works:**
- Record one short video each day (or let it run hands-free)
- AI selects your best moments and builds monthly highlight reels
- Browse by day, month, or let AI surprise you with curated categories
- Share albums with friends and family via iCloud

**Features:**
- 📅 **Daily Calendar View** — Jump to any day and watch that moment
- 🎬 **Monthly Reels** — AI-generated highlight compilations
- 🏷️ **Smart Categories** — Moments organized by scene type, mood, and people
- 👥 **Shared Albums** — Collaborate and comment with anyone via iCloud
- 🌙 **Dark-First Design** — Beautiful in low light, ready when you are

**Privacy-first:** All videos stored locally on your Mac. Shared albums use end-to-end encrypted iCloud.

### Video Preview Specs
- **Format:** H.264, 30fps, stereo audio
- **Aspect:** 16:9
